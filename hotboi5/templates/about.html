  • What is this about?

    Due to COVID19 people are trapped in their homes and they need to eat.
    This service allows for a user to simply put in items from their pantry and fridge and we automatically generate recipes that you can cook with them!

  • How does it work?

    After you send us your current ingredients, we scour the internet automatically looking for recipes that can be made with your current items and list any remaining items you may need!

  • What if I'm not sure what something is?

    If you are unsure about an item you have, upload a picture and using google vision API we can identify it for you and find recipes all the same!
